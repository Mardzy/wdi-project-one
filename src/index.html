Game Rules

Dice Sums, a game with 5 dice, and 3 rounds versus the computer. In each round 3 dice or less can be exchanged. Click the dice you would like to replace. Exchange dice will roll new dice to replace your selections. Click start, and your dice will be shown on the blue container. If you want to keep the dice you have, click hold, and sums will be compared. The next round button starts a new match, while keeping score of wins an losses. Highest value is 6, lowest is 1. At the end of the game your sum will be compared against the Computers'.
